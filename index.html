Tips to Avoid Phishing Scams:

  • Double-check the email sender's address before clicking any links.
  • Avoid clicking on links or downloading attachments from unexpected emails.
  • Verify the legitimacy of a website by checking its URL and SSL certificate.
  • Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
  • Use strong and unique passwords for each of your accounts.
  • Regularly update your antivirus and anti-malware software.
  • If in doubt, contact the organization directly using official contact information.
